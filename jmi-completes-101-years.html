During her address, Akhtar congratulated all the authors for bringing out intellectual works inspite of the challenges faced during the pandemic. University Librarian Dr Tariq Ashraf in his welcome address emphasised that JMI has continuously excelled during the last 3 years and achieved success after success despite facing difficult situations.

It as emerged as one of the top ranking global university and brand equity of the university has phenomenally increased, he said, adding that the library is trying its best to reach out to every user and provide best possible resources and services. Dr. Sabiha Zaidi, Director, Premchand Archives and Literary Centre convened the book release programme. She highlighted the important role Premchand Archive is doing in preserving precious things related to Jamia and invited authors of these publications to deliberate on the importance of their works. Dr Shahid Mahdi shared his views on the works of authors and stressed that these books are valuable contribution of the authors. The session ended with a vote of thanks presented by Dr. Nazim Husain Al-Jafri, Registrar, JMI.
